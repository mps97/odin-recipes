Breakfast Burrito

Description

A fast DIY breakfast for the road that's made in minutes with scrambled egg, salsa, and cheese wrapped up in a tortilla.

Remember before starting, wash your hands! Cleanliness is the start of every kitchen journey.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons salsa
  • 1 slice reduced-fat american cheese
  • 1 tortilla

Steps

  1. Spray a cereal bowl with nonstick cooking spray. Crack the eggs into the bowl, add the salsa, and stir.
  2. Microwave on high for 1 minute, stir, and cook for another minute or until the mixture firms up.
  3. Place cheese in center of tortilla and top with egg mixture.
  4. Wrap it all up like a burrito and head for the car!
